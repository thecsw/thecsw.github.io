🥥 At least 2 years, 9 months, and 12 days ago

I don’t remember, but this figure must have been my first. It’s so clear to me.

On a bright Saturday, I went downtown to one of the gaming stores. Having just gone through the magic of Re:Zero, I found myself in a state of trance.

I never thought of getting a figure until I saw her. I wasn’t sure whether I Should I get it or not, I have definitely felt something within me.

I didn’t buy it and went back home. A day or two later, I found myself thinking about it too much, which showed that this chance should not be toyed with.

I sprinted downtown after classes, and there she was. The last one. How could I have known the path I was about to take myself on.
