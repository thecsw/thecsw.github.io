Many go and ask themselves, what is the meaning of life? Some spend their entire lifetimes to come up with an answer that they would feel good or smart about. Many spiral themselves into self-imposed depressions, worry without an end, and locking their minds into a constant state of misery. All from a question on life’s meaning. Is it impossible to answer? Some would say so. Is the answer supposed to be something grand, beautiful, and divine? That is what we can assume from the effort that goes into it. Albert Camus said - "The meaning of life is whatever prevents you from killing yourself". Franz Kafka proclaimed - "The meaning of life is that it ends". I would like to assist my dear authors with an answer that is like finest things we have - simple.
