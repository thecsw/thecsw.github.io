I simply love it. Something about it. I always had a soft spot for cognacs, since my parents are Hennessy believers. With my lower than regular sugar levels, I like all stuff sour and tart. Can’t go wrong with this.

  • 1 1/2 ounces of Courvoisier VS cognac (from the freezer)

  • 1 ounce of Cointreau

  • 3/4 ounces of fresh lemon juice

Shake well and strain into a Nick & Nora glass. Voila. It will drive itself towards the person you’re most fond of.

Enjoy.
