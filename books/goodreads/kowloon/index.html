Melancholic, Gloomy, Yet Incredibly Captivating

An interesting story of a truly generic romance in a nostalgic land of Kowloon Walled City. The pacing and ambience that the story takes you in is weirdly soothing. It strikes that niche I always knew I craved for, but couldn’t put into words for so long. Kowloon Generic Romance is different from regular manga, as it focuses more on the adult romance and worries that come with it. Refreshing and intriguing. I’m in. ◼︎
