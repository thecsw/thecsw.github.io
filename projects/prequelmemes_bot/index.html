When I was a wee lad in high school, I made a Reddit bot that listened to any new submissions on the r/prequelmemes, apply OCR, and it shows the exact timestamp of when any found quotes appear in the prequel trilogy of Star Wars. I really liked prequelmemes back in the day. This project has long been unmaintained and shut down after it successfully accumulated over 50k karma.
