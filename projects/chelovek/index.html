In the Spring of 2021, I took one of the best courses ever! This was RUSS 712, a graduate class in Russian Literature. We read all the beautiful short stories by such masters like Pushkin (Blizzard), Gogol (The Overcoat) Tolstoy (Blizzard), Chekhov (The Lady with the Dog), Dostoevsky (The Dream of a Ridiculous Man), and dozens more. We would progress ourselves into modern times, so starting from 19th century and ending up somewhere in 2010s.

As a final project for the course, we had to make a 15-minute recorded video of us talking in TED-talk style about a research topic of our choice.
